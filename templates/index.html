Welcome to the "Set Time Generator" v.01

Purpose:
The "Set Time Generator" is designed to simplify and automate the scheduling of set times for events. By factoring in individual DJ preferences and past schedules, the app ensures everyone gets a fair chance to play at different times, such as early, middle, and late sets.

Instructions:
1. Enter your DJ name.
2. Select your set time preferences in order. For example: 1st choice: 3:30pm, 2nd choice: 4:30pm, and so forth.
3. Click "Submit".
4. Once all DJs have submitted their preferences, the app will generate and display the final schedule.

Thanks for using our app, and happy scheduling!
- Mark
